Postgresql

  • phppgadmin
1

$ cp /etc/apache2/conf.d/phppgadmin /etc/apache2/conf-enabled/phppgadmin.conf
  • 建立使用者
1
2

$ sudo -u postgres createuser --superuser $USER

2

$ sudo -u postgres createdb $USER

1

$ pg_dump -s > outfile
